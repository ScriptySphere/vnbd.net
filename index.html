Volunteer Network of Bangladesh

VNBD is an open community space where people share voluntary impact, experiences, ideas, and reflections related to Bangladesh.

Join the network See what VNBD is

VNBD is not a charity, not a fundraising platform, and not a service delivery organisation.

Why VNBD exists

Across Bangladesh, countless individuals contribute voluntarily. Many contributions remain undocumented and disconnected. VNBD exists to recognise, connect, and amplify these efforts through shared stories and responsible dialogue.

About VNBD

A voluntary impact and idea sharing network for Bangladesh.

What VNBD is

  • A voluntary impact and idea sharing network
  • A community driven dialogue platform
  • An inclusive space open to all citizens

What VNBD is not

  • Not a charity or NGO
  • Not a fundraising or donation platform
  • Not a political or partisan initiative
  • Not a service delivery or project implementation body

What VNBD aims to do

Simple, responsible, community centred.

Share impact

Create a common platform for voluntary impact sharing so stories and lessons are not lost.

Encourage reflection

Encourage reflection and learning from lived experiences through respectful conversation.

Inspire civic responsibility

Inspire civic responsibility through shared stories, ideas, and practical perspectives.

Foster dialogue

Foster respectful dialogue across diverse backgrounds, with careful community moderation.

Build shared memory

Curate learnings and archive resources so future volunteers can build on what came before.

Stay independent

Remain non commercial and non partisan while staying open to broad civic participation.

Organisational structure

Designed to be flexible, non hierarchical, and facilitative.

Initiator and founding circle

Vision stewardship. Safeguards the founding values and supports coordination without authority.

Core coordination team

Platform facilitation and coordination. Maintains standards and supports transparency.

Community moderation team

Safe space and community care. Moderates discussions and addresses misinformation or abuse.

Content and documentation team

Knowledge and impact curation. Curates stories, documents learnings, and maintains archives.

Outreach and engagement team

Community growth and participation. Engages contributors and supports communication.

Open contributors

Idea and impact contributors. Share stories, join discussions, and propose improvements.

Decision making

VNBD follows a consensus based decision making approach guided by dialogue, mutual respect, transparency, and shared understanding.
